About the Museum

MonoMuse features rotating exhibitions, permanent collections, and special events year-round. Founded in 2008 in downtown Pittsburgh.

Our museum spans four wings, each dedicated to a different aspect of contemporary art and technology. From interactive digital installations to curated analog archives, there is something for every kind of visitor. We also offer guided tours, educational workshops, and membership programs.
