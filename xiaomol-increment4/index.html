Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity. Through immersive exhibitions, interactive installations, and rotating collections, MonoMuse invites visitors to discover how digital innovation is transforming the way we create, experience, and understand art. Whether you are a first-time visitor or a returning member, our exhibitions offer new perspectives on the role of technology in culture and everyday life.

MonoMuse features rotating exhibitions, permanent collections, and special events year-round.

Our museum spans four wings, each dedicated to a different aspect of contemporary art and technology. From interactive digital installations to curated analog archives, there is something for every kind of visitor. We also offer guided tours, educational workshops, and membership programs designed to deepen your connection with the art world. Visit us any day of the week and discover what inspires you.
