Our Mission

MonoMuse is dedicated to exploring the evolving relationship between art, technology, and society. We create spaces where visitors can engage deeply with contemporary ideas through immersive, interactive experiences.

Who We Serve

  • First-time visitors curious about contemporary art
  • Students and educators seeking immersive learning experiences
  • Members who return regularly for new exhibitions and events
  • Families looking for creative, hands-on activities

Founding Year

MonoMuse was founded in 2008 with a vision to make contemporary art and technology accessible to everyone.

Milestones

  • 2008 — Museum founded in downtown Pittsburgh
  • 2012 — East Wing expansion opens
  • 2017 — Launch of the Digital Futures permanent collection
  • 2023 — Online ticketing system launched
